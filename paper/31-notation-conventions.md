### Index Conventions

- Greek indices $\mu, \nu, \rho, \sigma$: Spacetime (0,1,2,3)
- Latin indices $a, b, c, d$: Local Lorentz (0,1,2,3)
- Latin indices $i, j, k$: Spatial only (1,2,3)
- Repeated indices: Einstein summation convention

### Fourier Conventions

- Fourier transform: $\tilde{f}(k) = \int d^3x \, e^{-ik \cdot x} f(x)$
- Power spectrum: $\langle \tilde{f}(k)\tilde{f}^*(k') \rangle = (2\pi)^3 \delta^3(k-k') P(k)$
